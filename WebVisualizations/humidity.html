Humidity

The majority of cities surveyed have a humidity of over 60%, at least at the time of year this data was gathered. The lowest humidity, 0%, appears to occur only at the northernmost latitudes. We do not, however, find the same to be true for the southernmost latitudes. Again, this may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time this data was collected.
